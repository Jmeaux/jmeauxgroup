About Me

I love what I do

Having worked on a series of projects for multiple online businesses I have acquired a skillset for essentials in Shopify e-commerce platforms, mobile responsive web development, lead-generation webpages, and many more.

I was bitten by the coding bug at a young age. Once I learned that I can type a few lines on the screen and make things happen, I was hooked. Being a natural logical thinker, the first computer programming class I took was computer logic and I was very good at it. This let me know that I was choosing the right career path for me.

"I love doing this stuff! The more and more I work with people, the more I fall in love with helping people."

I am fueled by my passion for understanding the nuances of business. I consider myself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on my academic foundations in business and stay in tune with the latest strategies through continued coursework and professional development.

The many years of experience has given me great communication, problem solving, and organizational skills.

As a very detail oriented person, I go above and beyond to make sure I get the results desired.
